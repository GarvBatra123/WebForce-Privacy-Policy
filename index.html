Privacy Policy

WebForce • Last updated: 2025-09-26

WebForce (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Device & Diagnostics. Non-personal, aggregated data such as device model, OS version, crash logs, and basic usage analytics may be collected via platform services (e.g., Google Play) to improve performance and stability.
  • User Settings (On‑Device). Preferences you configure (e.g., trigger modes, vibration, overlays, URLs) are stored locally on your device. Unless explicitly stated, these settings are not transmitted to our servers.
  • No Personal Identifiers. We do not request or collect your name, email, contacts, photos, precise location, or other personally identifiable information.

How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functionality and remember your in‑app settings.
  • To maintain and improve performance, reliability, and security.
  • To analyze crashes and fix bugs.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent user data.
  • Limited diagnostic data may be processed by service providers (e.g., Google Play Services, Firebase Crashlytics if enabled) solely to provide analytics and crash reporting.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the data processed. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You may change or reset your in‑app settings at any time within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored preferences from your device.

International Use

Your device may send diagnostics to services that operate globally; their processing locations may vary according to their own policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised “Last updated” date.
